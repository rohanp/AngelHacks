Introducing crowdsourcing groceries

Our Mission

Cilantro is all about convenience. Let's say it's Friday night and you have a hankering for some sizzling stir fry. You could go to the store yourself, but that costs time and your favorite show just came on :( . You could order online... if you want to wait three days for that stir fry. OR you could just ask Alexa to open up Cilantro!

How it works

Using Amazon Echo, you can add all of your groceries to a list through Cilantro. Once you've added all of the items you need, all you have to do is publish your list, and let us do the rest. Cilantro will find the closest prospective driver to you and send them the items that you need. Then the drive can grab those items on the go and deliver them right to your door! Alternatively, if you are looking to pick up some quick cash, you can sign up to be a driver and get paid just to go grocery shopping!
